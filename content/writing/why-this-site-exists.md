Most personal websites are either resumes in disguise or abandoned blogs. I
wanted something closer to a workbench — a place I'd actually open, not just
link to.

The rules I'm giving myself:

- **Load fast.** One HTML file, one CSS block, one font. No frameworks, no
  trackers, no cookie banners to dismiss.
- **Write for myself first.** If a post is useful to someone else, good. If
  not, it still did its job by forcing me to finish a thought.
- **Finish things badly if that's the only way to finish them.** A rough note
  beats an unpublished draft every time.

That's it. The rest is just markdown and patience.
